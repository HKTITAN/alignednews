I was born in London, UK, and now live in Los Angeles, CA.

I founded [Los Feliz Engineering](https://lfe.org), a consumer software company named after the first neighbourhood I moved to in the U.S. We created [Honk](https://honk.me), a real-time messaging app, and [Family](https://family.co), a self-custody crypto wallet. In September 2023, LFE was acquired by [Aave Labs](https://aave.com), where I served as CPO until October 2025.

I currently work at [SpaceX](https://spacex.com) / [xAI](https://x.ai), where I lead design for [X](https://x.com). Previously, I was Head of Design at [Base](https://base.org), a division of Coinbase.

I’m also a co-founder at [Dip](https://dip.org), which creates and publishes tools for achieving interface excellence, such as [cmdk](https://www.npmjs.com/package/cmdk) and [Agentation](https://agentation.com).

I consider myself a designer at heart and enjoy building highly polished products.
